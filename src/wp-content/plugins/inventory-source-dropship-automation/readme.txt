Automate drop shipping through your WooCommerce Store by integrating directly into your dropship supplier's inventory.



== Description ==



Automate your Dropship Inventory Management in your WooCommerce Store or Wordpress Blog.  Inventory Source allows you to choose from 100+ Integrated Dropship Suppliers (or your own) to integrate directly into your website. Our daily monitoring ensures you are only selling products that in are in stock and our powerful automation tools allow you to bulk manage thousands of supplier products to only sell the items you want.



== Installation ==



Upload the Inventory Source: Dropship Automation plugin to your blog, Activate it then you'll need to sign up at InventorySource.com to add suppliers and distributors to your automated data feed. 

1, 2, 3: You're done!
